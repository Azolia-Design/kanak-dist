Sustainable Packaging Strategies for a Better Environment

What we did

This well-known BBQ brand needed packaging that would maintain product integrity and enhance brand presence. Kanak Naturals responded with custom-vented printed clamshells that showcased the brand's commitment to user experience, quality, and image. They also wanted sustainable packaging, so we developed products with no added PFAS formula while ensuring the grease repellant was very strong.

Results

The innovative packaging solution led to increased sales driven by improved brand visibility and product functionality.
